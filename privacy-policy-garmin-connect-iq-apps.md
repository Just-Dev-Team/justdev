# Privacy Policy — Garmin Connect IQ Apps

**Last updated: 2026-03-23**

## Overview

This privacy policy applies to all Connect IQ applications and widgets ("Apps") published by this developer for Garmin devices. Our Apps are utility tools designed to extend the functionality of Garmin wearables, such as displaying sensor data, environmental readings, and device information.

## Data Collection

**Our Apps do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

Specifically:
- No user accounts or registration required
- No data is sent to any server or third party
- No analytics or crash reporting
- No location data collected
- No advertising identifiers used

## On-Device Data Processing

Any data processed by our Apps — such as sensor readings, BLE advertisement packets, or device metrics — is handled **entirely on your Garmin device**:
- Data is used only to render the current display
- Nothing is stored beyond the current session
- Nothing leaves the device

## App Settings

Settings configured via Garmin Connect Mobile are stored locally on your device by the Garmin Connect IQ framework. Our Apps do not have independent access to your Garmin account or profile.

## Third-Party Services

Our Apps do not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs beyond the Garmin Connect IQ platform.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date.
